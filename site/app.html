About

Void Network Lite is a fully static fork of Void Network. There's no login, no account, no server-side anything. Just games.

How it works

This is a single HTML file. Every other file it needs — theme, scripts, images, the game manifest, and the games themselves — is loaded from jsDelivr, a free CDN that serves files from public GitHub repos. There's no backend.

What changed from the full Void Network

  • Removed accounts, login, and PRO tier — this build has no users.
  • Removed the proxy / unblocker stack (epoxy, scramjet, BYOD).
  • Removed Discord-link generators and chat features.
  • Kept search, category filter, recently-played, and favorites — all stored locally in your browser.

DMCA Takedown Policy

Void Network Lite respects the intellectual property rights of others. If you believe content hosted via this site infringes on your copyright, please send a complete DMCA notice with all of the following:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed.
  • Identification of the specific game URL on this site (e.g. #/play/42).
  • Your full name, address, telephone number, and email.
  • A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner.
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in the notice is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the owner.
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

Where to send notices

Email DMCA notices to the contact address listed in the source repository's README.

Repeat infringers

It is our policy to disable access to material that is the subject of a valid DMCA notice and to remove repeat infringers' submissions.

Privacy

Void Network Lite is a static site. We don't run a backend, we don't have user accounts, and we don't ask for any personal information.

Local storage

Your browser stores your favorites, recently-played games, and preferred category locally under localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Analytics

The site loads Google Analytics 4 to count anonymous page views. See Google's privacy policy for details.

Advertising

Banners and pop ads are served by Adsterra family networks (highperformanceformat.com, profitablecpmratenetwork.com).

Game content

Games are loaded from jsDelivr and may make their own network requests.
