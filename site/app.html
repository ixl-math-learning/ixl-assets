About

Void Network Lite is the static, no-account build of Void Network. No logins, no proxies, no PRO tier. Open the page, pick a game, play.

How it works

Every game on the site is loaded from jsDelivr, which serves files straight off public GitHub repos. Nothing here runs on a server we own.

What's missing on purpose

  • Accounts, login, PRO tier — none of it. There's no user state.
  • Proxy / unblocker stack — gone. Play games, that's it.
  • Discord-link generators, chat, BYOD domains — gone.
  • Search, category filter, recently-played, favorites — kept, but local to your browser.

DMCA Takedown Policy

If you own the rights to something hosted via this site and you want it taken down, send a notice that includes:

  • The work you claim is being infringed.
  • The specific URL on this site (for example #/play/42).
  • Your name, address, phone, email.
  • A good-faith statement that the use isn't authorized.
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that the notice is accurate and that you're authorized to act for the rights holder.
  • Your signature.

Where to send it

The contact address is in the README of the source repository.

Repeat offenders

Repeat infringers' submissions are removed.

Counter-notice

If you think a takedown was wrong, you can submit a counter-notice with the same identifying info plus a statement consenting to jurisdiction.

Privacy

This is a static site. There's no backend, no accounts, and no personal info collected by us.

Local storage

Favorites, recently-played, and your category choice live in localStorage. They never leave your device. Clear them from your browser settings whenever.

Analytics

Google Analytics 4 is loaded for anonymous traffic counts. See Google's privacy policy. Block it in your browser if you'd rather not be counted.

Ads

Banners and pop ads are served by Adsterra (highperformanceformat.com, profitablecpmratenetwork.com). They use cookies / similar tech.

Game content

Games stream through jsDelivr and may make their own network calls.
